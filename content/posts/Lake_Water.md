I am a big fan of organic pools. But not a big fan of murky lakes. 

After practicing triathlon swims in murky lakes, and racing in them too, I can say its one of the things I don't like about the sport. 

I do like my local pool. Even though in the summer it has bugs, debris and the sting of sunscreen water.

I don't like the geese deposits, the duty that dogs, cows and inflated prairie dogs floating like ruby balls. 

But I still want my own organic mini pond to swim in as I feel I could control the algae, slime and know whats in the deep ends.
