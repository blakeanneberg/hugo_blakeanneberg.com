Having a bike share right outside is great in the morning. Often there are many bikes, crowding out the bike stand. They are all pedal assist e-bikes. 

The first bit is choosing what route to go. Either under a bridge and across the roundabout circles or up and over the bike bridge. 

The bridge is good in the morning, as it feels faster and its not as cold. 

The roundabout and river are good in the afternoon as its cooler and the river is lit up by the sun. 

I would love to have my own ebike. But using the bike share is fine for now.

After the bike commute, I try doing Boot.Dev study. I also want to try and do a lesson in GameDev.TV. But the lesson on Boot.Dev is hard, and I have to take my time. 

So I should wake up earlier and get them done faster.
