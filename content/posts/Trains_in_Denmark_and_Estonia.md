---
title: "Trains in Denmark and Estonia"
date: 2026-05-13T11:23:14-06:00
---
My train journey last week in Denmark: 
- Copenhagen to Odense
- 104 miles
- Max Speed ~200 km/h
- 1½ hours
- 488 Danish Kroner/$76.48 round trip
- Both seat assignments.
- Very easy, somewhat busier, clean. 10/10 would recommend.

My train journey today in Estonia: 
- Tallinn to Tartu
- 118 miles
- Max speed approx 129 km/h
- ~2:25hours
- €48/$56 round trip
- No seat assignments
- So easy, quiet and clean. 10/10 would recommend.

I am so happy to have taken two trains. The Odense train was more of a proper train, while the train to Tartu seemed more like intercity train.

I wish there were both kinds of trains in Colorado.
