One thing I want to do is capture long form audio and video of places on the mountain. It will just be nature sounds and nothing else. 

One reason I want to do this is that it would be neat to have this as a looping video to watch.

The other reason is to capture all the seasons, so you can have a record of what it sounds like for each season.

It might be nice to have morning, midday, and  afternoon sounds.

I also want to perhaps include the river sounds.

This will help with any future video game, to make it feel rooted in environment.

I know of a couple locations on the mountain to do this, and some will be lower elevation near the river, some will be near a creek, and then some will be high up on a ridge, and then above treeline. 

Will be nice to do this, just need to charge up my camera with shotgun mic, lav mics and  get out there.
