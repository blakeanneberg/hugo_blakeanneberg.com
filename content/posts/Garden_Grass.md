With the extreme drought and lack of rain or snow, I have been looking into alternatives to Kentucky Bluegrass.

First I thought white clover, then micro clover and fescue mix, then flowering mix, and even buckwheat. But then I came across Yarrow. I have seen this one before and I love its fern like leaves. 

So now I think I want pretty much all these. Gone are the days for the bluegrass. Long live Yarrow.
