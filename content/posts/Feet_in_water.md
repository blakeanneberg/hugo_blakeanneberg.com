- Apply Godot learnings into the game.
- River is right though town, and I also like creeks.

- The sound of rivers are really relaxing. 
- I saw a fact that 90% of Colorado plants and wildlife are dependant on rivers. 
- Mountain streams are very much under appreciated and rated. 
- There are not enough video games about rivers. 
- I can use the same game engine for a game about skiing.   

Anyway, I am very much looking forward to it.
